ABOUT US

Our company serves as a host for several law firms wishing to conduct focus groups. The purpose of these focus groups is for the law firms to get feedback on cases that are going to trial. A moderator will be presenting the evidence as they would in court and then prompting discussion from the participants.



TWe are currently looking for participants from the following areas:

  • Baton Rouge
  • New Orleans
  • Metairie
  • Shreveport
  • Lafayette
  • Baton Rouge

Participants will be paid $100 each for a three hour session and catering will be provided. To apply, navigate to our sign-up page to complete the form and we will contact you with a specific date and location a week before the session you have been selected for.
