What do we do?

US Focus Groups primarily services law firms that are interested in hearing feedback about their cases before they go to trial. This feedback is often elicited from participants with the use of focus groups. For the typical focus group, a moderator will present the facts and evidence of a case as it will be presented in trial and prompt discussion from the group regarding their opinions on the case.




Focus Group Info

Focus groups are conducted both through Zoom and in-person. The average focus group will last for three hours and participants will receive compensation of $100 for participating.

If you are interested in participating in a focus group, please navigate to our sign-up page and complete the form. If you are selected for an upcoming focus group, we will reach out to check your availability and send additional information regarding the specifics of the focus group.
