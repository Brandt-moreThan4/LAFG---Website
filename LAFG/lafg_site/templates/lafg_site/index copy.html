What do we do?

US Focus Groups primarily serves as the middle man for law firms interested in hearing feedback on their cases before going through the trial process. We gather that feedback through online surveys and focus groups, conducted by the moderators from our team, through which we hope to gauge how people really feel about the lawsuits happening in their communities.


How do you sign up?

All we need is for you to fill out the sign-up form and we will work to place you into one of the upcoming focus groups in your area as they become available.




Focus Group Information

Focus groups are conducted both through Zoom and in-person. The average focus group will last for three hours and participants will receive compensation of $100 for participating.

For the typical focus group, the moderator will present the facts and evidence of a case as it will be presented in trial and prompt discussion from the group regarding their opinions on the case.


For more information about our focus groups, please check our Frequently Asked Questions page.
