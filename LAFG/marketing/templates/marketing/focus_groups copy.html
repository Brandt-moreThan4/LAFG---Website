• Timing: what date will you need to have the focus group done by?
  • Participants: how many participants will you need for this group?
  • Location: do you need the participants to be pulled from a specific area?
  • Platform: do you want the focus group to be conducted in-person or over Zoom?

  • Moderator: will you be moderating the focus group yourself or will you use one of our moderators?

    Some reasons you should consider using one of our moderators:

    • Moderating focus groups is not the same as lawyering
    • Unbiased
    • Experienced at pulling information and extracting input from all participants
    • Knows how to handle dominant or unruly participants.
    • Keep discussion moving
    • Interpretation of feedback is a valuable part of the process
  • Presentation: do you have a presentation ready, or will you need our assistance in compiling the facts into a focus group ready presentation?
